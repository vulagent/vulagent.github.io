What is VulAgent?

VulAgent is a vulnerability-mining tool powered by generative artificial intelligence (AI) that specializes in discovering integer-overflow bugs. During mining, it automatically creates proof-of-concept (PoC) inputs to trigger the flaws and, upon successful activation, produces detailed vulnerability reports.
